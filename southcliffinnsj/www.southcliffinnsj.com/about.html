Established in 1987, South Cliff Inn is an award-winning bed & breakfast that overlooks Lake Michigan. In 2018 the inn was completely renovated to a new coastal feel.

The inn sits on a private bluff overlooking Lake Michigan with breathtaking views and colorful sunsets. It houses seven units with private bathrooms over the three levels of the historic home, with some offering Jacuzzi tubs and fireplaces in all. Start your day with a homemade breakfast while enjoying the gorgeous perennial garden.

All rooms renovated in 2018 offering a beautiful coastal vibe offering a haven for relaxation. Each room has its own style so check out what suits you best. Almost all rooms offer a complete or partial view of Lake Michigan. Dining and game room have a complete, sweeping view of Lake Michigan, as well as the multiple decks that are available for guest enjoyment.

Nearby you can find many great activities, including:
 
  • Beautiful beaches: Lions Park ¼ mile; Silver Beach 1 mile
     
  • Quaint downtown Saint Joseph, MI 1 mile away
     
  • Many famous wineries within 15 minutes
